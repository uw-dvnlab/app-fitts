Welcome to the Fitt's task!

Follow the steps below to use the interface:
  1. Use the settings panel on the right to:
    • use the sliders to adjust the target distance and size
    • measure the yellow calibration line with a ruler and enter the length measurement in centimeters
    • enter the trial duration
  2. To record a trial:
    • click the "START" button
    • place cursor on central black dot and wait for the "GO!" message
    • move to the green target dots as fast as possible
      • each successful hit is +1 to the score
    • avoid the zones beyond each target (past the red lines)
      • each invalid zone entry is -5 to the score
    • upon trial time completion, view the final score
  3. To save all trial data, click the "Save Data" button in the bottom right
